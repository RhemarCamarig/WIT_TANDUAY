About Tanduay





Tanduay Rhum is one of the oldest and most iconic rum brands in the Philippines. Its rich history dates back to the 19th century, making it a significant part of Filipino drinking culture.



Tanduays story began in 1854 when a Spanish distillery in Hagonoy, Bulacan started producing rum using sugarcane.
